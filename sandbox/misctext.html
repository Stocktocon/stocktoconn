In guilty gear we use numberpad notation to notate inputs and moves. Numberpad notation uses numbers 123456789 to represent the cardinal and diagonal directions. The number represents one of the 8 cardinal or diagonals based on their placement on the numberpad. There's one exception. 5 represents a neutral movement input, meaning no movement keys pressed. but for example, 8 is up, 9 is up forward, 6 is up, 2 is down, 1 is down back, 4 is back. A basic quarter circle forward input could be notated as 236, meaning down, down+forward, forward.
More reading can be found at the Dustloop Number Notation Guide page.
